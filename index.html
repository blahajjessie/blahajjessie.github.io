Who am I?

My name is Jessie Srinivas. I care about tech, education, and the internet. Ocassionally, I write about things that I have an opinion about on my medium blog. I also like to cook, and have some recipies on my recipies website.

Why is my site like this?

I am nostalgic for the "bad design" of the 90's internet. The internet is bland nowadays, dominated by how much people focus on blending in and modern design. I wanted to do something different. I wanted to make a website that reminded people of the old internet, where people made pages to publish their stuff, and their websites were whatever they wanted. Full of horrible animations, crazy colors and a complete lack of graphic design. But hey, at least they requried barely any code!
